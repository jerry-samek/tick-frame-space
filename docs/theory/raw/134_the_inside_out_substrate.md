## Abstract

The framework's core, stated sharply: **the tick is not time.** A tick is one *causal operation* — "one call of a recursive function." Causality (the call/dependency DAG: what-calls-what) is fundamental; **time is the observer's read of causal depth, and space is the observer's merge of its incoming channels.** Both are inside-out reconstructions, not substrate containers. The substrate is *monist* — one shared causal structure (RAW 116/122's "one"); observers are *forks* of it, and an observer is a **self-maintaining, self-subtracting bundle of N channels that merges to one** (an eye is ~N receptors merged, not a point).

Two halves of the verdict, both earned this session:

1. **The ontology is theorem-backed, not crank.** "Causal order fundamental, time/space derived" is the founding thesis of causal set theory, and the **Malament/Hawking-King-McCarthy theorem** makes our depth/breadth split literal: proper time = longest chain, space = inextendible antichain. "Perceived dimension = correlation-rank of the merged channels" is **Cao-Carroll-Michalakis 2017 ("Space from Hilbert Space")** — and already the project's own **V3 ch003** (dimension = rank of the latency Gram matrix via MDS). So the core mechanisms are established physics in computational vocabulary.

2. **The framework's distinctive *escapes* from the locality/manifoldlikeness wall were refuted.** Memoization-as-source-of-locality and observer-merge-as-escape both **relocate** the wall, they do not dissolve it (§5, §12). Two independent literature passes converged on the same answer: **locality/manifoldlikeness is a *dynamically/energetically selected* property — a geometrogenesis / area-law-ground-state phenomenon — not something any kinematic rule or observer-side trick produces.** That open problem is shared with all of discrete quantum gravity; it is not a defect of this framework.

The genuine novel seam (§7) is narrow and real: a **localized, self-maintaining, self-subtracting N-tap convergence bundle** as the observer on a non-manifold DAG, with its perceived rank fixed by stability-selection — a fusion the literature search could not locate, but "the novelty is in the join, which is exactly where the unpaid debt sits."

The landing (§6): **physics is the goal, it can be objective — but objective the way relativity meant it (invariance across observer-views, grounded in the shared substrate), never the way Newton's absolute space meant it (the view from outside).** The god-view is impossible; that is not a barrier to objective physics, because objective physics was never the god-view — it is the invariants under change-of-observer. This *unifies* the "ontology vs. physics" question: **the physics is the ontology's invariant skeleton under change-of-fork.**

## 1. The starting reframe: tick ≠ time

Doc 50 said "time is a special amplifying generator." This RAW sharpens it: **the tick is not time at all.** A tick is one causal operation — *one call of a recursive function*. Causality — the call/dependency DAG — is the fundamental structure. Time is what an embedded observer reads off the *depth* of that structure; space is what it reads off the *breadth*. Neither is a container the substrate carries; both are renderings.

The recursion image is load-bearing:
- **tick** = one recursive call.
- **forking** = a call spawning sub-calls (independent sub-streams; anti-solipsism built in).
- **re-meeting / loops** = two call-paths reaching the same sub-problem and merging (memoization / state-identification).

This is the same even-handed monism as Doc 49 ("space as emergent visualization"), now applied to time as well: *both* space and time are projections of one recursive causal DAG.

## 2. What is EARNED (theorem-backed), and where we sit

- **Time/space from causal order — a theorem.** Malament / Hawking-King-McCarthy: the bare causal order fixes the conformal/light-cone structure; counting fixes volume ("Order + Number = Geometry," Bombelli-Lee-Meyer-Sorkin 1987). In causal-set language, **proper time = longest chain** (our "depth"), **space = inextendible antichain** (our "breadth"). So claims 1–2 are not new in kind — they are causal sets in computational vocabulary, and they come *for free*.
- **Dimension from correlation-rank — published, and already ours.** "Perceived dimension = best-fit dimension of the channels' mutual-information graph (MDS)" is **Cao-Carroll-Michalakis 2017**, and it is the project's own **V3 ch003** (latency Gram matrix → MDS). A multi-channel *merge* is structurally a **tensor-network contraction** (Swingle/MERA), its effective dimension set by correlation/bond rank.
- **Where the framework sits:** the causal-order-fundamental wing of discrete QG — **causal sets** (Rideout-Sorkin classical sequential growth = our "forking"), the **Wolfram model** (Gorard: tick = update event, time = causal-graph foliation, space = spatial hypergraph, re-meeting = multiway state-merge / causal invariance — the single closest program), and **relational time** (Rovelli "Forget Time", Page-Wootters, Markopoulou's internal observer). The distinctive axiom — time as fundamentally anisotropic (Doc 50, ρ=2.0) — has its mainstream home in **Hořava-Lifshitz** (z>1, d_s = 1 + D/z) and **CDT** (a time-foliation is what rescues a finite-d manifold from the tree/expander phases).

**This placement is the good news: the ontology is serious company, much of it literally theorem.**

## 3. The observer: monism and the merge-to-one

An observer (an eye) is **not a point**. It is a bundle of N "single observers," each tapping one branch/pipeline, each processing its arriving deposit-stream; the observer **merges** them, **self-subtracting** its own contribution (or it reads only itself — the "frozen planet" bug, Exp 64_109 v21; RAW 132 §3.5).

This resolves the binding/homunculus worry Tom raised ("you can't observe N — only one or all"):
- "One" and "all" are not rival options; they are the **two ends of one recursive merge** — a call consumes *all* its sub-calls and returns *one* value. You never observe N; you observe the **one the merge of N returns**, which is itself one channel into the next merge up.
- **No homunculus.** The "one" is the *return value*, not an inner watcher (Dennett's "no Cartesian theater"). The unified self is a real *construct* — the call's current return — not a fundamental observer needing its own observer. The regress stops because the return *is* the unity.
- The formal neighbor is **Integrated Information Theory** (Tononi): Φ measures the degree to which N elements form *one* irreducible whole; IIT's "exclusion postulate" is precisely "you observe one or all."

And it does not collapse to solipsism — it collapses to **monism**, which the framework already is (RAW 116 single entity, RAW 122 the one, RAW 038 observer-separated forks). Solipsism: "only my mind exists." Monism: "there is one existence; I and all others are real forks of it." You are one fork, observing through your merge-to-one; others are equally real, forks of the *same one*, sharing the *same substrate* your cone overlaps. Metaphysical solipsism stays unfalsifiable from inside any viewpoint (the human condition, not a framework defect) — and the framework's *answer* to it is the monist axiom, the same "one" it already takes as the irreducible floor (RAW 122). Not a proof; the floor, met from the inside.

## 4. Time and space from the inside

From "you read one tick after another, from your position, and stop only when the stream stops," a phenomenology falls out — and it is *earned*, not assumed:

- **Time = the read.** The arrow of time *is* the order of the recursive evaluation (you get call N's return before N+1's; no random access). RAW 044's "lag of reality," exactly.
- **The future is unseeable because it is not yet computed.** The recursion hasn't reached it; there is nothing there to read. A cleaner account of the open future than any flowing-time story.
- **You can't stop until the stream stops.** You don't *have* experiences and choose to continue — you *are* the reading; a stream-processor runs while its input flows. Doc 28's renewal-not-persistence, first-person.
- **Space = the merge** (the N channels combined into one "now"); **time = the sequence of merges.** Neither is a container.
- **"Now" = the past light cone.** Because the N channels reach *different depths* back toward the root, each merged "now" is a layered cross-section of the past (near channels = recent past, deep = ancient), all at once — exactly the relativistic now (the sun 8 minutes ago, the stars centuries ago, one glance).
- **Horizon.** You cannot see all the way to the root; the origin sits at or beyond the horizon (cosmological-horizon structure; RAW 026 falls out).
- **Death = your fork's stream halting, while the one persists** (monist reading).
- **The god-view is structurally impossible.** No fork can read except sequentially, from its position. The whole-at-once vantage isn't *available to anyone* — being an observer just *is* being unable to occupy it. (This is why the 118–136 arc kept erring: it described a viewpoint that, in this ontology, cannot exist.)

## 5. The wall: manifoldlikeness / locality is a *dynamical selection*, not a rule

This is the hard, honest core. The question "does the substrate yield a finite-dimensional manifold" hits a wall that the framework's distinctive moves **relocate but do not escape.**

- **Exp 136 (the locality wall):** a local, conservative, symmetric, coordinateless substrate gives only an **expander** (no locality) or a **tree** (hyperbolic). Finite-d needs a *neighborhood structure on the distinctions* — which is the geometry we refused to assume.
- **Causality-fundamental inherits it.** Generic causal-set growth → Kleitman-Rothschild "pancakes" (non-manifold); escape is only *dynamical* (Benincasa-Dowker-Glaser action). **Bolognesi (arXiv:1004.3128, 2011.12174) already built causal sets from the DAGs of recursive computations and found no generic manifold (d≈3.5).** Our exact idea has a prior negative.
- **Memoization is the knife-edge, not the escape** (§12.1). Content-keyed merging is blind to causal distance: global content-equality → an **expander** (= Wolfram's branchial space, documented non-manifold); fine-grained → a **tree**. The manifold is a knife-edge "causality is fundamental" does not pick; landing on it requires a neighborhood/window = smuggled locality.
- **The observer-merge relocates, it does not escape** (§12.2). The merge only *reports* the rank it is handed (theorem: Naor distortion bound; Pope "intrinsic dimension tracks input"). On **volume-law / expander** channels it returns garbage (= our NAND-cloud, = Exp 65); it returns ~3 *only* when the channels are **area-law**. So the wall moves from "the graph" to "the channels," renamed **area-law vs volume-law**. And the empirical prior is against the rescue: real biological merges *expand* intrinsic dimension (V2 cortex ~3D→~7D, *Science Advances* 2023), so "stable observers are ~3D, therefore see ~3D" is not automatic.

**The convergence (the hardest-won result of the session):** two independent literature passes — causality-fundamental and observer-merge — landed on the *same* verdict. **Locality / manifoldlikeness is energetically/dynamically *selected* (an area-law ground-state / geometrogenesis phenomenon), not produced by any kinematic rule or observer-side trick.** Every distinctive move relocates to the same unpaid bill: **what dynamics selects area-law locality?** This is the open frontier of the whole field (Carlip; Benincasa-Dowker-Glaser; Quantum Graphity geometrogenesis, whose leftover "disordered locality" = our NAND-cloud), not a flaw unique to us.

## 6. Objectivity as invariance, not god-view (the landing)

If physics must be built from what the observer sees, and the god-view is impossible, **can physics be objective?** Yes — but objectivity was never the god-view.

Special relativity already *killed* the "view from nowhere": it abandoned absolute space, time, and simultaneity (god-view fictions) and relocated objectivity into the **invariants** — what all observers agree on despite seeing differently (the interval, c, proper time, rest mass, curvature). **Objectivity = invariance across observer-frames, not a frame-free absolute.** So "go from what the observer sees" is not anti-objective; it is the foundational move relativity already made, pushed to the substrate.

For this framework: monism gives the one shared substrate (something real to agree *about*); the forks give the observers; **objective physics is precisely what is invariant under change-of-fork** — what every inside-out merge agrees on because they read the same one. Not a god-view; an invariance — real, and reachable from inside, which the god-view never was.

This dissolves the observer-relativity-of-dimension worry (RAW 110): dimension may be **frame-relative, like length** in relativity, with objectivity hiding one level down in an invariant of the causal structure — and "why do all our frames agree on ~3D?" gets the **selection** answer (only stable observers persist; they land in the same regime; 3 is objective as *invariant across the class of observers that can exist*).

And it **unifies the ontology-vs-physics fork**: the physics *is* the ontology's invariants under change-of-observer. Relativity = invariants under change-of-inertial-frame; this program = invariants under change-of-fork. "Build the ontology" and "get to physics" are not two destinations — the physics is the invariant skeleton of the ontology.

## 7. The genuine novel seam (honest)

The literature search could not locate one specific fusion: a **physical, localized, self-maintaining, self-subtracting N-tap convergence bundle** as the observer, on a *non-manifold* causal DAG, whose perceived rank is fixed by **stability-selection** (only self-maintaining patterns persist). Causal sets / Wolfram / CCM / Van Raamsdonk all compute geometry *god-view* over all factors; the observer-side papers (Correlationhedron, arXiv:2502.13293) use a single probe or abstract slicing, not a physically re-converging *persistent pattern*. That is the white space.

**Honest caveat, twice over:** (1) "the novelty is in the join, which is exactly where the unpaid debt sits" — the new part is the part that has to pay the geometrogenesis bill. (2) The supporting precedent for "identification creates loops" is the identical-particle orbifold (quotient → braid group) — but that quotients an *existing* manifold; it does not make a low-d manifold *from scratch*.

## 8. Falsifiable claims / banked test program

The ontology mostly *explains structure* (phenomenology, relativistic observation) rather than *predicting numbers*. The places it could genuinely *bite* on physics, banked for a future (coding) session:

1. **The participation-ratio test of the observer-merge** (pre-registered):
   - Build an N-tap bundle; measure the participation ratio `D_PR` of the channel-correlation matrix.
   - *Test 1:* on an **expander**, `D_PR` must come out **high** — if it comes out ~3, you measured a prior, not emergence.
   - *Test 2:* **expander vs 3D-lattice** — ~3 on both = smuggled merge-prior; ~3 only on the lattice = honest.
   - *Test 3 (the real test of stability-selection / claim 5):* on Exp 134, do **survivors** (self-maintaining patterns) cluster at low `D_PR`, or — the falsifier — **expand**?
   - *Test 4:* two overlapping bundles must agree on the geometry (the objectivity-as-invariance check).
   - Specify self-subtraction as explicit *whitening*.
2. **The past-light-cone test:** does "depth-seen vs distance" in the merge reproduce the **light-cone / invariant-interval** relation quantitatively? (Needs the metric → needs locality → loops back to the wall, but it is the sharpest "bite.")
3. **Geometrogenesis:** is there a weighting/action over call-histories under which the *area-law / locally-regular* pattern **dominates** the sum, with directed-time as the symmetry-breaker? This is the field's open question, stated in tick-frame terms.

## 9. What this does and does NOT claim

## 12. Wrong Turns and Rejected Ideas

- **12.1 Memoization as the source of locality — REFUTED.** Content-keyed merging is causal-distance-blind: global content-equality → expander (= branchial space, non-manifold); fine-grained → tree. "Locality for free" is analytically dead; only defensible as "buy locality via content-identification, *pay with Lorentz invariance*."
- **12.2 The observer-merge as an escape from the wall — REFUTED.** The merge only reports rank (Naor/Pope); on volume-law/expander channels → garbage. It returns ~3 only if channels are already area-law. So it *relocates* the wall to the channels; "the manifold is in the eye" is false-for-free.
- **12.3 Claim 5 (dimensional closure selects observer-rank ≈ 3) — asserted, empirically challenged.** Real biological merges *expand* intrinsic dimension (3→7). Selection may bias toward stable architectures, but it does not follow that perceived rank = architecture dimension. Needs its own argument; banked as Test 3 (§8).
- **12.4 "We are moving toward the past" — corrected.** You move *toward the future* (frontier grows) but *look toward the past* (read toward root): movement forward, gaze backward. The literal phrasing flips it; the underlying insight (observation is backward-directed) is right.
- **12.5 The recurring god-view slip — named.** Repeatedly we analyzed "the graph" from outside (its d_s, manifoldlikeness). That viewpoint is *structurally unavailable to any observer*; it is not just bad method, it cannot exist in this ontology.
- **12.6 trit → 3 global axes — softened.** Held as one hypothesis, not load-bearing; replaced by "dimension is a local function of the connection structure" (RAW 110), with the value of 3 as a selection/invariance outcome, not a derivation.
