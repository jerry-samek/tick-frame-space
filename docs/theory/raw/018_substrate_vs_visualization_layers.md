| **Relativistic Effects** | None at substrate (causal invariants preserved)              | Emergent artifacts: time dilation, length contraction, infinite energy             |
| **Role**                 | Fundamental causal substrate; true physics                   | Perceptual layer; visualization of substrate evolution                             |
