## How to Read Archived Documents

Documents in `archive/` used geometric substrate assumptions that are now superseded.
However, many **conclusions** survive — the logic may be sound even if the substrate
description is wrong. Look for:

- Logical/information-theoretic arguments (substrate-independent)
- Process descriptions (entity behavior, observer behavior)
- Experimental results (valid for their specific substrate, mechanism may transfer)

Documents in `review/` are transitional — probably valid but not yet audited.
