1. The photon is **not** a propagating entity.
2. The photon is a **degenerate temporal process** that spends its entire tick budget countering expansion.
3. The gamma field is the **actual propagating structure**.

This resolves all classical paradoxes associated with photons.

## **2. Definitions**

### **2.1 Photon (P)**
A photon is a **stationary imprint** in the gamma field defined by:

- **zero internal evolution** (no free tick budget)
- **zero spatial expansion** (does not follow cosmic expansion)
- **zero proper time** (no internal ticks)
- **zero mass** (no momentum budget)
- **infinite lag** relative to expanding entities

Formally:

\[
P(t+1) = P(t)
\]

The photon is **temporally present** but **spatially fixed**.

---

### **2.2 Gamma Field (Γ)**
The gamma field is the **substrate-level smoothing field** that:

- expands with the universe
- propagates disturbances at the sample-rate limit (c)
- carries oscillatory modes (wavelengths)
- transports information about stationary imprints

Formally:

\[
\Gamma(t+1) = S(\Gamma(t))
\]

where \(S\) is the smoothing operator.

---

### **2.3 Gamma Imprint (γ‑imprint)**
A gamma imprint is the **persistent deformation** in Γ caused by an entity’s presence or transition.

A photon is the **minimal possible imprint**.

## **3. The Degenerate Process Principle**

### **3.1 Statement**

A photon is a **degenerate temporal process** that spends its entire tick budget countering the expansion of the gamma field.

Thus:

- it cannot evolve
- it cannot move
- it cannot expand
- it cannot accumulate energy
- it cannot experience time

This is the only known process with **zero net expansion**.

---

### **3.2 Consequence: No Motion**

Because the photon uses all ticks to resist expansion:

\[
\Delta x = 0
\]

It is **stationary** in the substrate frame.

---

### **3.3 Consequence: No Proper Time**

Because the photon performs no internal updates:

\[
\tau_P = 0
\]

This matches the classical statement “a photon experiences no time,” but here it is derived from first principles.

\text{Physics interprets } \Gamma(t+1) - \Gamma(t) \text{ as } P \text{ motion.}
