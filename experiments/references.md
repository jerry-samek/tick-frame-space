## Detailed Integration Notes

### Interferometry (Foundational)

These experiments validate the coherence assumptions underlying the Temporal Dynamics Principle.

- Cronin et al. (2009) — definitive review of atom/molecule interferometry
- Hariharan (2003) — optical interferometry fundamentals
- Loudon (2000) — coherence, beam splitters, photon interference
- Arndt et al. (1999) — landmark demonstration of C60 interference

Use these when discussing phase accumulation, coherence length, discrete-time phase sensitivity, or interferometric
predictions of the tick‑frame model.

---

### Collapse-Model Experimental Tests

These directly constrain GRW/CSL/DP parameters.

- Vinante et al. (2017) — ultracold cantilever experiment; strong CSL bounds
- Curceanu et al. (2016) — spontaneous X-ray emission tests
- Bahrami et al. (2014) — optomechanical CSL tests
- Nimmrichter & Hornberger (2013) — macroscopicity measure

Use these when comparing tick‑frame predictions to CSL, discussing empirical viability, or analyzing collapse-induced
noise.

---

### Macroscopic Quantum Experiments

These probe the boundary between classical and quantum behavior.

- Eibenberger et al. (2013) — interference of 10,000 amu molecules
- Romero-Isart (2011) — levitated-sphere superpositions
- Delic et al. (2020) — ground-state cooling of a levitated nanoparticle

Use these when discussing macroscopicity, proposing experimental falsification, or comparing tick‑frame decoherence to
standard QM.
